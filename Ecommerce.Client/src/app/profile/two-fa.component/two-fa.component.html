Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security to your account by requiring a verification code in addition to your password.

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your account. After entering your password, you will be asked for a one-time verification code.

2FA Status {{ is2FAEnabled ? 'Enabled' : 'Disabled' }}

Benefits of 2FA

  • Prevents unauthorized access to your account even if your password is compromised
  • Protects your sensitive personal and financial information
  • Adds an additional verification step that only you can complete
  • Highly recommended for accounts with sensitive data or payment information
